Cereal For Adult Children: Oreo O's

What made you want to be an adult when you were a kid? Was it the thought of being able to drive? Not needing permission to have sleepovers? Or maybe it was the idea that you could stay home all day and play video games, because you'd be out of school and therefore have no other responsibilities besides becoming the best Halo player in the world? Unfortunately, for a lot of us, the adult world hasn't been as carefree as our child-selves may have envisioned. Fortunately, however, Post Consumer Brands knew exactly what we needed and gave us Oreo O's! This cereal is for the kid at each of our hearts. No "grown-ups" to tell us we can't have dessert for breakfast. Let's get our day started the right way, regardless of what our physician says!

Ingredients:

  • Oreo O's
  • Milk
  • A childlike view of the world

  1. Pour Oreo O's into empty bowl (or milk into an empty bowl *shudders*

Slutty Brownies Recipe

When Slutty Meets Taste

Where the name originated and why, who knows--I personally would have gone with something akin to Frankenstein's Monster. Nonetheless, Slutty Brownies are an easy, layered dessert that combine three other desserts that can easily stand alone: chocolate chip cookie dough, Oreo cookies, and brownies. These are my personal favorite, so if you are looking for the perfect dessert to gorge yourself on or impress your friends at the next get together, you have to try this Slutty Brownie recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Brownie Mix
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Water
  • Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough
  • Oreo Cookies

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. Spray a 9" x 13" baking pan with non-stick spray
  3. Add the brownie mix, eggs, vegetable oil, and water together in a bowl. Mix and set aside
  4. Press the cookie dough into the pan in an even layer
  5. Layer the Oreo Cookies on top of the cookie dough
  6. Pour the brownie batter on top of the Oreo Cookies and spread evenly
  7. Bake for 35-45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted into the center of the brownies
  8. Remove the pan from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes before slicing
  9. Serve by itself or with ice cream or any of your other favorite toppings
  10. ENJOY!
